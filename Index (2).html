Sumner in the City National Convention 2026

Welcome to the official page for the 2026 Sumner in the City All Class Reunion, September 18th-20th. This site brings together reunion details, registration information, pricing, and the weekend agenda in one place.

Association focus

The Sumner High School Alumni Association preserves the legacy and spirit of Sumner by keeping alumni connected through events, outreach, and community partnerships. Rooted in pride, scholarship, and fellowship, the association celebrates alumni achievements and strengthens Spartan identity across generations in Kansas City, Kansas.

Graduate services

Built around alumni connection

Standing together, we are ensuring Sumner graduates have ongoing support, information, and opportunities to stay engaged with one another and the broader community. Through communication, events, and outreach, these services help alumni maintain strong ties to Sumner’s legacy and to fellow Spartans across generations.

Agenda

The convention schedule remains visible for attendees planning Friday, Saturday, and Sunday activities.

Friday, Sept. 18

Kickoff events

  • 10:00 a.m. Dedication of the new SHSAA memorial marker at Douglass Elementary School.
  • 10:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m. Chester Owens SHSAA Alumni Room Tour at Sumner Academy of Arts & Science.
  • 12:00 p.m. – 7:00 p.m. Vendors room open at Margaritaville Hotel.
  • 2:00 p.m. – 7:00 p.m. Registration at Margaritaville Hotel.
  • 6:00 p.m. – 9:00 p.m. Meet and Greet for registered guests.
  • 9:00 p.m. – 11:00 p.m. Hospitality suite for registered guests.
Saturday, Sept. 19

Main convention day

  • 9:00 a.m. SHSAA General Meeting.
  • 10:00 a.m. – 11:00 a.m. Workshops 1 and 2.
  • 11:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m. Workshops 3 and 4.
  • 4:30 p.m. – 5:15 p.m. Class pictures for the 40s/50s, 60s, and 70s including 79 and 80 attendees.
  • 6:00 p.m. – 8:30 p.m. Dinner and Awards Banquet.
  • 8:30 p.m. – 11:30 p.m. Dance.
Sunday, Sept. 20

Closing worship

  • 11:00 a.m. Worship service at First Baptist Church of Quindaro, 3030 Stacy Hopkins Ave., Kansas City, KS 66104.
  • No cost All are welcome to attend the Sunday service.
